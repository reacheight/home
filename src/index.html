Hi, I'm Roman and this is my little portfolio and a tribute to the game I love.

I started playing CS 1.6 when I was around 12 years old, and this game had me hooked. At some point, I even googled 'how to become a pro CS player' and read a sarcastic article that suggested eating only buterbrods.
So as you can guess, I didn't end up as a CS pro. But I still ended up having so many happy hours playing this game and so many fond memories. Playing it will always feel like I'm 12 again - it's a sunny Saturday morning, no school, mom's doing laundry, and all I have to worry about is how to frag more.

I hope this site brings you some nostalgia and maybe even makes you want to play CS 1.6 again.

You can find my basic info and contacts in the 'Options' window and my projects in 'Find servers'.
